Why is the sky blue?
The sky is blue due to Rayleigh scattering — blue light scatters more through air molecules than other colors in sunlight.
How big is the Sun?
The Sun is about 1.4 million km wide — over 100 Earths could fit across it! It makes up 99.8% of our solar system's mass.
What is photosynthesis?
Photosynthesis is how plants make food using sunlight, water, and CO₂. They produce glucose and release oxygen as a byproduct!
